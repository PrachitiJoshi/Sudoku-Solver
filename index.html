About Sudoku


Sudoku is the one of the puzzle games whose popularity is increasing day by day.A japanese puzzle game Sudoku is based on the logical placement of numbers. An online game does not require any calculations nor special math skills; all that is needed are brains and concentration. This game is being loved by people from any age group. In this game we have a grid in which we have to fill the digits within 1 to 9 in such a way that no digit repeats in a row,in a column or in a particular grid section itself. It needs logic to complete the whole grid. Here we are providing you with our version of sudoku puzzle.We hope you will definately enjoy.
Happy Playing!!



How to play


  1. Click on below Start button to start.
  2. Set timer and level for the game.
  3. Click on GetPuzzle button to get a sudoku puzzle of specified level.
  4. Choose the appropriate number to input to particular box in grid.
  5. In case you want new puzzle in between,click on NewGame button.
  6. Timer will show the remaining time.
  7. You have 5 lives,you lose a life when you enter incorrect number in the grid.
  8. If you are unable to solve puzzle,we are providing you solution which you can see using Solution button.
